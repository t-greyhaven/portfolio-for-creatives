  • About

    Greets! I am Tolan Greyhaven a Designer and Illustrator based in Vancouver, BC. Equipped with insatiable curiosity and a deep respect of complex problems, I’m ready to collaborate on developing solutions for both digital and physical environments.

    A passionate extreme sports enthusiast, you’ll find me spending lots of his free time on the move. Mountain Biking, Gravel Cycling, Skateboarding, Snowboarding, and I’m learning how to ride Horses!

    When I find space for more stillness I enjoy well crafted stories (especially in books), playing Magic the Gathering, and working on my illustrative skills.
