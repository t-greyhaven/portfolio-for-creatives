CommTrack

Managers often face challenges in communication training and skill development for young employees who are used to doing everything on their preferred digital platforms. CommTrack features give employees the freedom to manage their own onboarding and learning process with clear channels for progress check-ins.

CommTrack empowers young employees with self-paced onboarding while keeping managers connected through structured feedback and progress tracking. It simplifies training, making skill development more efficient and engaging.
